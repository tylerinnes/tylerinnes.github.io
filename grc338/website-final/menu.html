Dinner Menu

At Harvest & Table, we serve comforting, modern American dishes made with fresh, honest ingredients. Our menu blends familiar favorites with simple, thoughtful updates — hearty sandwiches, bright salads, and shareable plates crafted for any appetite. Good food should feel welcoming and effortless, and that’s exactly what we aim to deliver.
